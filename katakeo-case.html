Katakeo


As part of the Awesome Inc Web Development Bootcamp, we were tasked with creating from scratch and demoing to an audience a capstone project. Four months of rigorous study and practice culminating in a two-week sprint to produce our final project. I knew I'd be spending a lot of hours on this, so I decided on a topic that was both interesting to me and would be helpful to someone else. Called Katakeo, from the Greek word for 'instruction,' because Sunday mornings I teach a middle-school confirmation class and I wanted to build an engaging way to help students with their memorization work.
