A bit about me.

After spending a decade working full time in the outdoor recreation industry, guiding folks of all walks of life in the backcountry and in a dozen different countries, I'm now pursuing making a difference in the tech industry. My main focus is in development, project management, and user experience design.

In the spring of 2019, I took an introduction to web development course, thoroughly enjoyed it, and in the fall I went all in and joined the full-stack Web Development Bootcamp provided by Awesome Inc.
Check out some of my projects completed recently, as well as links to my LinkedIn, GitHub, and email.

If you would like to learn more about me or are looking for some web development help, don't hesitate to reach out!
I am more of an "in-person" type of guy, so I'd also be happy to grab a coffee (or brew) with you.
