A bit about me.

Hello World! My name is Todd and I am in the process of making a career transition from outdoor guide to web and software developer.

After spending a decade working full time in the outdoor recreation industry, guiding folks of all walks of life in the backcountry, I began looking into careers that would be friendlier towards family life. The outdoor industry is extremely rewarding, but also very taxing on being present for the kiddos.

After a conversation with a friend, I began to seriously consider the tech industry. After all, technology is found in every field, it highly values creativity, and could provide a little more stability in the scheduling realm.
In the spring of 2019, I took an introduction to web development course, thoroughly enjoyed it, and in the fall I went all in and joined the full-stack Web Development Bootcamp provided by Awesome Inc.

Below you can check out some of my projects completed in the last year, and in the navbar you'll find links to my LinkedIn, GitHub, and email.

If you would like to learn more about me or are looking for some web development help, don't hesitate to reach out!
I am more of an "in-person" type of guy, so I'd also be happy to grab a coffee (or brew) with you.
